Investment Philosophy

Where the
Wealth Is Moving

01
Find the Transfer Before It Happens
The AI revolution is not creating wealth evenly. It is concentrating it at specific chokepoints: infrastructure, compute, energy, and the software layer underneath. We identify those chokepoints before the crowd does and build conviction while the price still reflects doubt.
02
Conviction Built From the Source
We read what others skip. Every position starts inside the filings, not from a headline. Capital structure, dilution schedules, covenant language, related-party transactions. The edge lives in material that is publicly available but rarely read by anyone with the patience to understand it.
03
Hold Through the Noise
Asymmetric payoffs require asymmetric patience. The businesses we cover are mispriced because they are misunderstood, and misunderstandings resolve on their own timeline. We build a position, document the thesis, and let the fundamentals do the work while the market catches up.

About LAI

Independent
Research Group

Liberty Avenue Investment was built around a single observation: the largest wealth transfers in history happen quietly, in filings and footnotes, long before they make headlines.

The AI revolution is one of those moments. Capital is flowing at a scale not seen in a generation toward infrastructure, compute, and the companies that make it run. Most investors are watching the obvious names. We look at what the obvious names cannot function without.

Every thesis is built independently from primary sources. No sell-side. No consensus. No noise.
